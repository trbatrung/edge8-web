## About the Role

We're looking for an AI Engineer to build the automation systems and workflow templates that power our client work. You'll take business problems, map the data, and ship working AI solutions — not prototypes, not slide decks. Real systems that run.

## Key Responsibilities

- Build and optimize AI-powered automation systems and reusable workflow templates
- Enhance and simplify user experiences by connecting and orchestrating diverse tools and platforms
- Analyze business processes to uncover automation opportunities and translate them into scalable AI solutions and execution plans
- Design data architectures and integration strategies to ensure systems, workflows, and information remain connected and efficient
- Work alongside engineering, product, and business teams to develop, deploy, and refine AI automation initiatives

## Qualifications

- 3–5 years of experience with AI technologies and frameworks such as TensorFlow, PyTorch, scikit-learn, or similar
- Proficiency in Python, Java, or Scala, with strong SQL skills
- Hands-on experience in data engineering, data manipulation, and data preprocessing
- Excellent written and verbal communication skills in both English and Vietnamese

## Nice to Have

- Experience in mobile development, familiarity with APIs, UI/UX design, or database management
- Stays current with the latest advancements in AI tools and models
