## About the Role

We're looking for a Marketing Assistant to support operations with a focus on marketing, communications, and events. You'll work closely with the Chapter Manager to keep communications sharp, events running smoothly, and the community well-informed. The ideal candidate is organized, creative, and genuinely excited about using AI to work smarter.

## Key Responsibilities

**Marketing & Communications**
- Manage and schedule content across social media channels (Facebook, LinkedIn, Instagram, WhatsApp)
- Use AI to automate tasks and rapidly produce content
- Plan and execute marketing campaigns for events, initiatives, and major announcements
- Maintain and update the website with current information, events, and resources
- Create visual assets — graphics, posters, banners — using Canva or similar platforms
- Support monthly newsletters, event recaps, and internal communication updates

**Data & Documentation**
- Update and maintain member information, attendance data, and chapter trackers
- Collect and organize photos, media files, and event documents
- Prepare reports including membership, event, and communications metrics
- Ensure data accuracy across HubSpot, WhatsApp groups, and internal files

**Event Support**
- Assist with planning, logistics, and execution of events (learning events, socials, forum trainings, etc.)
- Coordinate suppliers, venues, materials, sign-in sheets, and member reminders
- Help manage on-site registration, check-in, and event experience

## Qualifications

- 1–3 years of experience in marketing, content creation, events, or admin roles
- Fluency in English with excellent written and verbal communication
- Comfortable using AI tools like ChatGPT and Gemini
- Ability to design simple visuals using Canva or similar tools
- Experience with social media management (Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn)
- Comfortable with spreadsheets, data entry, and organizing information
- Willingness to work flexible hours, including evenings and weekends when events require it

## Nice to Have

- Experience with HubSpot, Wix, or event planning platforms
