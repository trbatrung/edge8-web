## What it is

Edge8 is an AI consulting and global talent firm. It helps founders and CEOs install AI leadership, build AI programs, and staff their teams with Vietnam-based AI-trained talent. edge8.co is the lead-gen surface: every page routes to one consultation booking.

The point of view is simple. Most AI consultants talk theory. Edge8 builds. The frame on the site says it directly: "It's not an AI problem. It's the data." The company itself is the proof — Microsoft to Vinasource to TINYpulse to Edge8 is a real operator track, not a thought-leadership career.

## Values

Ranked.

1. **Be tech-forward, not tech-curious.** Implementation over theory. Ship work; don't pitch frameworks.
2. **Global talent is a force multiplier.** Vietnamese AI talent paired with international founders is the wedge.
3. **Build, don't pitch.** Case studies are the credential. Word-of-mouth from real installs beats paid acquisition.
4. **Lineage matters.** Microsoft + TINYpulse + Vinasource is the proof of operator credibility. Don't bury it.
5. **Calm.** One Typeform CTA. No funnel jujitsu. The work sells the work.

## Moat

- **The Vietnam talent pipeline.** Local recruiting (Mai Dang / Talent Edge) is the hard-to-replicate piece. Every placement deepens the network on both sides.
- **Operator lineage.** Microsoft → Vinasource → TINYpulse (acquired by WebMD) → Edge8 is a track record that consultants without it cannot manufacture.
- **The "Tech-Forward" frame plus 8-Steps framework.** Owned IP. The 8-Steps timeline is the spine of every consultation pitch.
- **Partner bench.** David Niu, Eric Enriquez, Jeff Hu, Bin Yu — each is a credible name attached to a real domain.

## What we are explicitly not building

- A general AI consultancy chasing every vertical (we serve mid-market founders)
- A pure recruiting agency (we place talent into systems we helped design)
- A SaaS product (Edge8 is a services + talent firm; AI Officer Institute is the productized adjacent brand)
- A content site (the blog feeds the funnel; it is not the business)
